Data Types

Let's open our file index.html and to do the following in the Chrome console.

Create person:

> var person = new Person({first_name: 'Anton', age: 19, hobby: 'Django developer'})

Let's look at the resulting person

> person
  Person {first_name: "Anton", age: 19, hobby: "Django developer", work: function}
> person.first_name
  "Anton"
> person.age
  19
> person.hobby
  "Django developer"
> person.work()
  "Anton is working."

Create another person

> var person2 = new Person({first_name: 'Us', age: 20, hobby: 'Android'})
> person2.work()
  "Us is working."
